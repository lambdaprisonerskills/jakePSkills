What We Do!

We seek to help forward-thinking inmates who are motivated to better and prepare themselves for their post-prison futures. The unemployment rate is tragically high for former inmates, so our goal is to give them a leg up. At the same time we give them the opportunity to develop and improve their skills while making some money along the way.

A Prisoner's Testimonial

"My prison job made me feel like I was fulfilling my existential duty to society: I was contributing. It doesn't surprise me that prison work assignments are credited with reducing recidivism. Any change for good that happened within me while I was incarcerated grew out of my job. If I feel that way about my time making chicken a la king, an inmate who's saving lives fighting fires must feel it 10 times over."
~ Anonymous

What we Believe

We believe there are a lot of prisoners out there who deserve second chances. Unfortunately, there is a huge societal stigma that has been cast onto all prisoners, making second chances seem impossible. We are here to break that stigma and to create mutually beneficial second chances.
